    it's been a long day without you, vulkaners!

    we are glad to invite you to take part in vulkan round 1 (div. 2) and vulkan round 1 (div. 1), which will start on friday, august 30, 2024 at 20:05 utc+5.5. you will be given 6 problems and 2 hours and 30 minutes to solve them in both divisions.

    starcoder for his excellent coordination and translations. codewiz, algorithmace, techguru, bytemaster, datasage, devhero, logiclancer, problemsolver, quickthinker, and debugninja for their invaluable testing and feedback. special thanks to techmaster for the vulkan and polygon platforms.

    hope you will enjoy the problems!

    new go toolchains added to vulkan testing servers

    by @techmaster - 1hr ago

    we are excited to announce the addition of Go toolchains to our vulkan testing servers!

    In response to the growing demand for support for various programming environments, we've integrated the latest Go toolchains into our server infrastructure. This update includes Go 1.21.2, ensuring compatibility with modern Go modules and enhancing performance during testing phases.

    New features include:

    • Enhanced performance benchmarks for Go applications
    • Improved support for concurrent programming and Go routines
    • Extended libraries and modules for advanced testing scenarios

    We believe this addition will provide a significant boost to the efficiency and accuracy of your submissions. Thank you to our team of developers and contributors for their ongoing support and feedback.

    happy coding!
